Email Tenancy Investigation Brief

Subject: Investigation into Cross-Tenancy Email Configuration Issues

Date: January 9, 2025

Prepared For: CTO

Overview of the Issue

The COO is unable to add the mailbox accounting@D2CustomGears.com to their Outlook client, which is already logged into accounting@ConseroInc.com. The root cause stems from the cross-tenancy setup:

  • Consero Inc. uses a Microsoft-sold Office 365 tenancy.
  • D2CustomGears uses a GoDaddy-provisioned Office 365 tenancy.
  • The current cross-tenancy configuration does not permit seamless authentication between the two environments.

Investigation Timeline

1. Review of Consero’s Microsoft 365 Tenancy

  • Accessed the Microsoft 365 Admin Center.
  • Verified all active mailboxes under the Consero tenancy. Result: No @D2CustomGears.com mailboxes exist within this tenancy.

2. Review of GoDaddy Office 365 Account

  • Logged into the GoDaddy account tied to Consero.
  • Found that the listed domains and mailboxes do not include D2CustomGears.com. Result: The D2CustomGears setup appears to be managed under a separate GoDaddy account.

3. Communication with the CEO

  • Action Required: CEO must provide CTO with GoDaddy credentials for the account governing D2CustomGears.com for investigation to proceed.
  • Supporting Evidence: The GoDaddy registrar of D2CustomGears.com was confirmed via WHOIS Lookup.

Findings

  1. The mailbox accounting@D2CustomGears.com is not configured under the Microsoft-sold Office 365 tenancy or the currently accessible GoDaddy account.
  2. The D2CustomGears.com domain and its mailboxes are actively in use by employees and accessible via web.
  3. A separate GoDaddy account, managed directly by the CEO, controls the D2CustomGears.com domain and mailboxes.

Immediate Actions Required

  1. Access the CEO’s GoDaddy Account: The CEO must provide credentials for the GoDaddy account managing D2CustomGears.com to proceed with further troubleshooting.
  2. Verify the Mailbox Exists: Confirm that accounting@D2CustomGears.com is active within the GoDaddy Office 365 tenancy and retrieve its login credentials.
  3. Set Up Temporary Forwarding: If immediate access to the mailbox cannot be established, configure forwarding from accounting@D2CustomGears.com to accounting@ConseroInc.com to ensure uninterrupted email delivery.
  4. Add the Mailbox Separately in Outlook: Once credentials are retrieved, add accounting@D2CustomGears.com as a separate account in Outlook.

Mid-Term Actions

  1. Document Current Tenancy Configurations: Create a map of all domains, accounts, and mailboxes managed by both Microsoft and GoDaddy, highlighting areas of overlap and fragmentation.
  2. Communicate Challenges to the CEO: Emphasize the operational inefficiencies caused by multiple tenancies and the risks of limited administrative access.

Long-Term Solution

Consolidate Tenancies

Migrate all mailboxes, domains, and accounts from GoDaddy’s Office 365 tenancy to the Microsoft-sold Office 365 tenancy.

  • Simplified administration and billing.
  • Elimination of cross-tenancy authentication issues.
  • Centralized user and mailbox management.

Conclusion

The investigation revealed that cross-tenancy restrictions and fragmented account management are the primary causes of this issue. Immediate action is required from the CEO to grant access to the GoDaddy account managing D2CustomGears.com. Consolidation of all tenancies under the Microsoft-sold Office 365 platform is recommended as a long-term solution.
